1. SETTING UP A USERNAME

If you’re just starting out as a creator on OnlyFans, then you’re probably not so familiar with the fact that you’ll have to set up a Username. However, if you’re already a creator or run similar pages on other platforms, you’ve probably nailed this already. #. SIMPLICITY: it’s one thing to set up a Username that sounds cool, and it’s another to remember this “set” username when promoting. A basic rule of thumb is to choose a simple username that can easily be remembered. Remember, not only are you going to be integrating this username everywhere you can and during promotions, but it also needs to be something your fans or followers can easily remember.

#. COORDINATION: Again, when setting up your unique USERNAME, you may opt to make it feel or look cooler by adding tons of hyphens and other symbols. Try to incorporate subtle identifiers and your niche so fans can easily know what you do and what you’re all about. A few features like body type, age, hair color, etc., Can be integrated into your username.

#. USERNAME CHANGE: while you’re after tweaking your username to one that follows the trends or sounds a lot better, we highly advise against changing your usernames regularly. If possible, use one username for the entirety of your OnlyFans career, as this username will be integrated into every post, promotion, and engagement. Additionally, this will confuse your fans if it’s changed every now and then.

You’ve got to understand that only a few sentences will be visible to people who see your page. So it’s best to keep it short and simple. However, you can still do a lot of advertising. You can easily tell people who view your profile to follow your handle like this, @name without leaving a link. Remember, every form of promotion is important.

Tip 2- your Bio serves a multitude of purposes. If you’re keeping your hungry fans expecting, you may as well let them know how often you will make content. This info can go in your Bio.

Tip- 3 Go all out! One thing people who aim to be successful on OnlyFans often neglect in their Bio is an “About you” section. This part is very important to your fans and your page. This is where you Include info about yourself and the type of content you produce. Plus, you can leave breadcrumbs here to reel in more subscribers. For example, why people should actually subscribe to your content. Be persuasive yet simple here.

3. Pinned Post

A pinned post is just like the one on every other platform. It lets you put out a certain post on your page, so everyone gets to see it at first glance without going through your entire profile.

Tip 1 – Sell out with ” Custom introductory Video.”

Not often do creators take advantage of the custom pinned post feature. To some, it may be recruiting their best post in a pinned post, which isn’t bad. But here’s a quick trick. Finally, make sure to pin this to your profile so everyone who goes through your page sees this first.

4. AMAZON WISHLIST

An Amazon wishlist is a list of items a creator plans to get. So if you’re eyeing up a certain product on Amazon, you can add this to your Wishlist. OnlyFans lets you share this list with your subscribers, so they can see what you wish for or like without having to read up about it or even ask you. 

Asides from tips on OnlyFans, subscribers, and fans can go the extra mile to get your gifts and stuff on your Amazon wish list.  

Tip 1- No commissions

Unlike subscriptions where OnlyFans gets to keep 20% of everything you make, an Amazon wishlist doesn’t carry the same terms. Fans and Subscribers can get you complimentary gifts as a show of appreciation or more like a tip, and you get to keep it all. Be sure to attach your Amazon wishlist to your OnlyFans account.

Tip 2- Edit your Security

One thing that could stand in as a bit of a disadvantage is the fact that while checking out, fans or subscribers may get to see your address which is your state and city. Even worse, having your address out there isn’t good for business. Plus, you don’t want fans trooping in and catching you unawares. So here’s what you can do.

Click on the link for <Gifts & Wishlists>. This is located at the top page above the search box. Go ahead and check if your address is visible by tapping  <See What They See>. This should be “Show Ship To: No Address Entered.” If it’s a different thing you find, then go back and click on <Manage This List> and then <Edit Settings> and select <None> before saving the settings. You’re now safe from people knowing your exact location by address.

OnlyFans allows creators to create promotions. Like every new creator just starting out on OnlyFans, gaining your first few subscribers may be difficult. One thing you can do is offer subscription-based promotions, which you can access through Edit Profile- Subscriptions.

Another great perk to play with is the subscription bundle feature. You can include subscription bundles for subscribers that aim to help them save more if they subscribe ahead of time. For example, say your subscription price stands at $8 per month. You can offer a three-month plan for $20. This way, your subscribers save $4. This works every time, and your subscribers will pay ahead most of the time. However, be sure not to overdo this.

6. SUBSCRIPTION PRICING

This is the price you set for your monthly subscriptions on OnlyFans. The minimum you can set for this is $5, while the max is $50.

Tip 1- Pick The Perfect Subscription Price

It’s one thing to set a price, and it’s another to get subscribers who actually pay that price for what it’s worth. A basic rule of thumb when just starting out with subscription prices is to set a price that fits the content you produce and your worth. A high price may drive away potential subscribers, while a low price may translate to low confidence on the creator’s part.
