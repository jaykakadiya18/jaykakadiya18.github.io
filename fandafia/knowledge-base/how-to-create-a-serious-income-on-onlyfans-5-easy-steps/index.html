How to Boost your Earnings on OnlyFans- 6 Tips

  • Subscription fees 

At the basic level, OnlyFans pays you through a monthly subscription model. OnlyFans’ minimum subscription fee is $4.99. You have the liberty to change the subscription fee up to about $49. OnlyFans takes a cut of 20% for every paying and recurring subscriber, and you keep 80% of the earnings. The more subscribers you have, the greater your earning potential. While calculating your net profit, include the applicable tax charges to your location. 

Before you decide on the price to charge for your content, research to determine the average market price, and you should price your content fairly. Many OnlyFans creators charge a flat subscription fee of $9.99. Ensure your pricing reflects the value and quality your content offers to the audience; else, you risk losing subscribers.

OnlyFans allows you to promote your discounted rates at any time. Use discounted subscriptions to encourage your initial audience to keep paying and to attract new consumers. Additionally, you can offer free trials before a subscriber starts to pay to attract new subscribers or retain your current ones. 

OnlyFans also provides you with the option of creating your affiliate program. You can offer a subscriber a free month of viewing your posts for every paying referral they get you. This is a good trade-off because you get to increase your fan base, earning you more. 

  • Tips 

Tipping is another excellent way to make money on OnlyFans. You can request your audience to tip you through private messages. You would want to encourage your viewers to tip you if they find your content valuable. Please do not make it seem mandatory because you are better off keeping your subscribers rather than pestering them to give you tips because they might unsubscribe. 

When you go live, take advantage of the tips, and host a session full of fun activities and quality content. Subscribers who feel touched by your content will tip you. Additionally, create a “Tip Menu” to collect tips even when you are not hosting a live session. This way, you can earn passively from your previous posts. 

  • Pay-per-view content and messages 

Pay-per-view messages conceal videos or images behind a paywall. The receiver must pay to view the content. This OnlyFans feature is an excellent way of selling exclusive content many of your subscribers requested. The minimum amount you can charge a minimum of $3 for every pay-per-view message you send. 

Use the pay-per-view messaging feature to request payment from fans who request custom videos and photos. For every positive response you get once you have delivered the custom content, keep in touch with the subscriber to see if they need more. 

OnlyFans provides you with a mass messaging system that you can use to promote your upcoming content to your current subscribers. Use this feature to ensure your current subscribers do not miss your content. You can also send time-limited pay-per-view messages. Time-limiting a message makes your subscribers curious, and they may become anxious about not wanting to miss your content, finally triggering them to pay to view the content. That way, you would make more income. 

  • Sponsored content 

Brands are always in search of endorsers. The only way to capture the attention of brands in your niche is by amassing a decent subscription of loyal fans. You are better off working with brands in the same market space as yours because your audience might have questions to verify why they should use the brand’s merchandise. 

 If you plan to endorse brands that do not resonate with the content you are producing, direct your audience to the brand’s website, where they can find answers to all the questions they may have. This way, you will not risk tarnishing your brand in case all does not go well for your subscribers as they use the brand’s products. 

  • Affiliate marketing 

Affiliate marketing refers to a referral program where you earn a commission for every subscriber you refer to a product, and they make a purchase. Some affiliate marketing programs only require that your referral click their link or sign up for a newsletter. This would be a great way to earn extra income without depending on the referral to purchase a product. 

Do research to find lucrative affiliate marketing programs such as ShareASale and Amazon Affiliates, select the product you would love to promote, get your link, and embed it within your OnlyFans content. You will earn based on the promotion terms for every product you promote. 

Before promoting a product, research whether it is legit or fraudulent; this ensures that your subscribers do not lose their money through your brand, protecting your brand’s image. 

Some affiliate programs allow you to earn for a lifetime once a referral makes a purchase. Look for these affiliate programs and promote the products so that you can have a recurring monthly gain for every subscriber who signs up for the program or purchases a product through your link. 

  • Live to stream 

OnlyFans offers you two live-streaming earning models. You can do this either through tips or gated streams. 

OnlyFans tipping system resembles that of Twitch’s bids. While you are streaming, your generous and entertained subscribers can tip you. To earn more tips, you can create a goal-led challenge, such as asking your audience to help you raise a specific amount of money for a good cause. 

Gate streams allow subscribers who have paid a fee to access your live stream. You collect a fee from every subscriber who successfully joins your meeting. To gain more from gated streams, hype your upcoming streams and give your subscribers an offer they cannot skip.
