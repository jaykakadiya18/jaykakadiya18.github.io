1. Let Your YouTube Profile do the Promoting 

Before a prospective subscriber decides to click on that subscribe and notification button, they are likely to first check out your profile. Your profile helps the prospective subscriber decide whether to subscribe and keep tabs on your upcoming content or not based on the information you provide on your profile. Bear in mind that your profile acts as a summary of what your YouTube channel is all about with the most impactful information being within the about section. Within the about section, you can provide a description of your channel, location details, and link to other websites. 

To write an effective description of your channel, you must first consider your target audience with reference to the content you post on OnlyFans. Create persona descriptions for your target audience to ensure the about section promotes your content to the right people on the fly. 

Persona descriptions contain details including the demographics and psychographics of your target audience. The demographics analysis describes the gender, age, income, and more about your prospective subscriber. A psychographics analysis looks into the opinions, personality outlook, lifestyle choices, and more about your target subscriber. You should research more to create a detailed persona description. 

The aim of creating a persona description is to help you understand your prospective subscriber and come up with the correct words to use within your description. Using words that appeal to your target audience makes them feel understood; therefore, giving your channel a chance by subscribing. 

After creating a persona description and recording a list of the words you can use to communicate to your target audience, you should now be able to write a worthy description. The description should outline the type of content you create, your objectives, and have a clear call to action. 

If your niche covers NSFW content, do not mention it within your profile; rather, use suggestive words that signal your target audience that you have an NSFW-oriented OnlyFans channel. For example, you can use a phrase such as, “Top 1%, can’t wait to see you on the other side. Let’s chat. Links in my profile.” You can also use suggestive emojis. Avoid mentioning the word OnlyFans in your description. 

Your objectives should be genuine with regard to the type of content you post on OnlyFans and YouTube. Remember, despite creating SFW or NSFW content, you should not mention that you have an OnlyFans account within your descriptions. The objectives should inform the prospective subscriber what they stand to benefit once they subscribe to your content. 

Your call to action should use compelling language with action words that clearly inform the potential subscriber what they are signing in for and what to do next. Keep in mind that the aim of the call to action is to help the future subscriber make a quick decision, so keep it simple, concise, and clear. But, do not sacrifice clarity for briefness.

2. Search Engine Optimize Your Content 

Other than creating valuable and quality YouTube content and avoiding various post mistakes, you should optimize your content so that your potential subscribers can find you easily on Youtube. YouTube is the second largest search engine used by numerous people seeking to solve various problems. They find their desired content by typing in different keywords within YouTube’s search area. Then, YouTube’s algorithm provides them with an array of videos they can select from and view. That is why you have to undertake keyword research before creating content. 

A keyword research process requires you to first decide the topic you would love to discuss. If you lack a topic of discussion, you must research to determine various questions asked by random users on the internet pointing in the direction of your niche. Once you have a precise topic, you should research to find the correct answers or discussion points you can use to elaborate your topic. By analyzing the discussion points and your topic of discussion, then can you determine the keywords to target. 

There are two types of keywords: short tail and long tail keywords. Short tail keywords are made of less than 3 words while long tail keywords have more than 3 words. Short tail keywords attract greater but less converting traffic while long tail keywords attract low but highly converting traffic. That is why you have to combine both of them to find a better balance of the amount of traffic you receive and the conversion rates.

For example, if you create fitness content on YouTube, a keyword such as “weight-loss” would be a short tail one, while one such as “Weight-loss by women suffering from stress eating” would be a long tail keyword. From this example, you can observe that the short tail keyword lacks a specific target audience while the long tail keyword has a specific target audience. 

Once you have made up your mind on the topic of discussion and collected enough discussion points, it becomes easier for you to outline the keywords you would love your content to be found for. Using the discussion points and the keywords list, you can then write highly effective and converting scripts. You should also include precise keywords within your captions and video descriptions. 

You can also leave a pinned comment on your video with precise keywords to boost the chances of your content being found. Take advantage of the capability to pin your comment on any video to include a message for your prospective subscribers with a clear call to action. You can also include a masked link directing those interested to your OnlyFans page. 

To increase the chances of your content ranking first for the keywords you are targeting, you should transcribe your videos and upload the file to YouTube. The transcribed text serves as captions or subtitles increasing the accessibility of your content. You can also include hashtags to improve the chances of your videos being found. 

Additionally, you should include catchy thumbnails on your videos. Thumbnails serve as the preview of what you have discussed within your videos. Once you have a great thumbnail to include within your videos, create a link card to direct your viewers to your OnlyFans pages.

Social media influencers are accomplished content creators, athletes, musicians, and more, who have garnered several followers who believe in what the influencer says. Paying an influencer to promote your YouTube channel to their audience can lead to a quick and tremendous growth of your channel.

Doing this benefits the influencer and you because the influencer gets to keep his or her fans while your channel receives genuine traffic.
