How to Select an OnlyFans Niche that Suits Your Lifestyle: 5 Ways

There is no right or wrong niche. Therefore, do not beat up yourself worrying about selecting a niche that may not work for you. Maybe you have been creating content for a niche that is not selling as expected: do not worry about that, you have the opportunity to select another one. You should have a mentality of testing out various niches until you find one that suits you well. But, do not skip from one niche to the other within a short period. Take time to experiment with one, about six months. 

The process of selecting a niche outlined within this article reduces the chances of you selecting a niche that may disappoint you because it focuses on your current lifestyle state and the subscriber-base your content would attract. 

Referencing your current lifestyle state allows you to create content for your selected niche without stressing or negatively impacting your daily routines. Therefore, you will have time to cater to your OnlyFans business while also running other errands. 

Selecting a niche with a description of your prospective subscribers in mind gives you peace of mind when creating content because you know who you are speaking to. You will have fun when creating content, learning, correcting mistakes, and building a relationship with your subscribers. There are high chances of you finding your niche from the start and never looking back if you follow this process:  

1. Review Your Passions and Interests 

Everyone has that one topic or activity they love the most. What’s yours? Maybe you love helping your friends solve relationship issues, cooking, secretly shooting bikini photos when you are at home, or more. Write down a list of the activities or topics you see yourself wanting to talk about and create content about on a long-term basis. 

If you find it hard to record your beloved topics or activities, consider what you like doing when you are free. When you are free, there are high chances that what you do is the one thing your body system loves to undertake. Such activities give you unforgettable satisfaction because they help you kill stress after a long day of undertaking various activities outlined within your schedule. For example, do you love trying out new recipes after a long day at work? This would be a great niche because it is something you do autonomously. 

You can also consider the topics you love reading about to come up with a list of your interests and passions. For example, politics, real estate, technology, and more. Listing a topic you love reading about gives you an advantage because you have enough knowledge and experience with the topic. You can comfortably answer your prospective subscribers’ questions, demonstrating credibility and authority. 

You can also review the organizations or clubs you are or have been a part of to create a list of your passions and interests. Think of the topics you love or loved contributing to the most to help your fellow members solve their problems. Think of the activities your club or organization got involved in and those that you loved. 

There are several other questions you can ask yourself to determine your interests and passions. Though, keep in mind that the aim of this exercise is to list everything so that you can easily prioritize the activities or topics you love the most. Once you have a priority list, you can proceed to the second step. 

2. Identify the Problems to Solve 

Now that you have a priority list, choose from among the top three a single interest or passion you want to focus on. You must select one so that you can proceed to study the problems surrounding your topic of interest.

Once you have selected a topic of interest, proceed to search for problems that people would love you to solve. To find the problems, you must reach out to your prospective audience or research on the web to find out what problems people are facing. 

The quickest way to determine the problems your prospective subscribers are facing is by doing keyword research. People use keywords to find information on the web using search engines. That is why you rely on keyword study to determine what your prospective subscribers might be asking on the web. By going through a list of the keywords they use, you can derive the problems they are facing. 

You do not have to carry out a keyword study manually. There are several tools, both paid and free on the internet that you can use. Such tools include Google Trends, Moz Keyword Explorer, Semrush, and more. These tools require you to input a topical keyword you want to research so that the tool can output questions and discussion topics that regular web users are seeking answers to on the web. 

You can also join various forums that align with your selected topic of interest to determine what questions the members of the forums ask. You can even run polls within forums asking the members questions regarding the problems they face. 

Without forgetting, you can have one-on-one conversations with different people including family, friends, or acquaintances to determine what problems they face with reference to the topic of interest you feel like you want to create content for. 

If you do find a significant number of problems that are new and yet to be solved, you can proceed to the third step, else, you should review your interests-and-passions priority list and select another topic of interest to determine the possible problems. 

3. Investigate Your Competition 

Your competition includes other OnlyFans creators who are creating content for the same topic of discussion you have selected. They already have a significant footprint in the market and know what to do, that is why you must study what they are doing to determine whether you have a chance of competing. 

Check out what problems your competition is solving. Since OnlyFans does not allow you to view the content of other creators without subscribing, you can rely on the information provided by the other creator within their profile. Check their profile to determine what they are offering to their audience. If some of your competitors do not charge a subscription fee, readily subscribe to their channel to see what they post. You can also go through the social media accounts of your competitors. 

It is reasonable to record the problems your competitors solve so that you do not end up creating content that solves a problem in the same way as that suggested by the other OnlyFans creator. This is repetition. You get to set yourself apart from the other creators by solving current problems faced by your prospective subscribers meaning your content would be original and unique. You also get to improve on the solution provided by the other creators earning you credit for the more optimized solution.  

If after your investigation you find out that all your listed problems have been solved, but you believe that you have better solutions, you should proceed to the next step. The same applies to when you find that there are numerous new problems yet to be solved. But, if you feel like your competitors have better solutions, you should reselect another topic of interest, create a list of problems, and investigate your competition again. 

4. Determine the Market Value of Your Niche 

So, are there people willing to spend on content that solves the problems you have found? Afterall, the aim of having an OnlyFans account is to sell your solutions to your prospective subscriber in the form of content. That is why you must know the profitability of your solutions to evaluate whether your time and efforts would be compensated fully. 

Look through your competitors OnlyFans accounts to determine the amount they charge for their content. Then, research to determine how much they bring in from their OnlyFans business. This move gives you a rough estimate of the amount your content might be worth. 

If possible, subscribe to your top five OnlyFans competitors to review how their sales are going. You get to know which of their content sales the most and whether you can create content capable of making more sales than your competitor. 

If you realize that your selected topic of interest is not selling, you should reconsider it, and go through step one, two, and three again. But, if your topic of interest is making sales, proceed to step five. Though, if you also find that your content is not selling and you are okay with it being that way, you can proceed to step five.

If you feel that you do not want to let go of a topic it means you are really passionate about it and are willing to share it to the world while hoping it makes sales. There are creators who have been able to monetize topics that are mostly free on numerous social media platforms such as comedy, you could be one of them.

5. Test Out Your Selected Topic of Interest 

Once you find a topic of interest that satisfies the requirements mentioned within phase two, three, and four, you must test the topic out by creating content. You should start creating quality and valuable content for your audience and promoting it on various social media platforms such as Twitter and Reddit. 

OnlyFans is a long-term venture: that is why you must take time to test a topic of interest before you settle on it. You can create and market your content for about six months before you decide whether the topic suits you. If your selected topic’s content does not sell, you can consider switching niches using the process outlined in this article or getting help from a great OnlyFans agency.

Selecting a profitable OnlyFans niche requires your utmost focus, time, and effort to analyze various aspects such as your interests or passions, the problems you can solve, what your competitors are doing, and the market value of your niche.
